About
Measures2Go lets clinicians quickly access Standard Outcome Measures from a mobile device, making tests like the Berg Balance Scale and Dynamic Gait Index simple and portable. Carry all of these measures in your pocket, eliminating the need for pen and paper and making it easy to change your assessment plan on the fly. Mark each assessment item-by-item using a simple and intuitive touch interface, and a score will be automatically generated and interpreted. With increasingly strict insurance requirements, make it seamless for yourself and your staff to capture patients’ progress objectively without using valuable treatment time.
